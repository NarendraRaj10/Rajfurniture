About Us

At Raj Furniture, we believe your home and workspace should reflect your style and comfort. Founded with the vision to offer high-quality, affordable furniture to every Indian household, we specialize in creating pieces that are both functional and stylish. Inspired by trusted brands like Nilkamal, Raj Furniture brings a fresh perspective to modern furniture design. From cozy sofas and elegant dining sets to durable beds and smart storage solutions — we cater to all your furnishing needs. Every piece of furniture we design is built with precision, crafted using top-grade materials, and delivered with care. Whether you’re furnishing your dream home or upgrading a single room, we’re here to make the experience seamless and satisfying.
